Elijah Piazza

I am from a military family, and was born in Louisiana, but ended up traveling every three years from Kansas, Alabama, Hawaii, and finally back to Alabama for good. I enjoy programming embedded systems, painting, playing Dungeons and Dragons as well as Warhammer 40K, and circuitry. I am also a member of the EHS Cyber Security Team, and National Honor Society. My certifications include HTML & CSS, Adobe Express, Digital Media Literacy, and Fiber Optic Technician Certification. I hope to obtain a bachelor's in Computer Engineering with a minor in Computer Science.

Sawyer McAlhany

Ever Since He was a young boy, Sawyer loved Computers, this passion eventually lead to his acceptance at The University Of Alabama with a CyberSecurity Major. His hobbies include Video Games, Tinkering, and Renewing older hardware. He is certified in CompTIA ITF+ and FANUC Handling Tool while also trying to get his Security+ This year.
